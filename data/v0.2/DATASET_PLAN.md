\# GaiaLab Naija Dataset v0.2 Plan



\## Goal



Expand the dataset from 100 to at least 500 carefully reviewed examples.



\## Target distribution



\- 150 English-to-Nigerian-Pidgin examples

\- 100 Nigerian-Pidgin-to-English examples

\- 100 customer-service examples

\- 100 Nigerian business-terminology examples

\- 50 professional business-writing examples



\## Quality rules



Each example must:



\- Preserve names, dates, quantities, and delivery times

\- Avoid inventing unsupported facts

\- Use natural Nigerian Pidgin where required

\- Give factually correct business definitions

\- Follow the requested tone and format

\- Avoid unsafe legal, financial, medical, or regulatory claims



\## Evaluation priorities



The v0.2 model will be tested for:



\- Translation accuracy

\- Detail preservation

\- Hallucination rate

\- Business terminology accuracy

\- Nigerian Pidgin naturalness

\- Instruction following
