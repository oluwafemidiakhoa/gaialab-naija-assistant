# Data licence

The 20 records in `data/sample_training_data.jsonl` and the six prompts in
`evaluation/benchmark.jsonl` were written originally for this repository. To the
extent possible under law, the contributors dedicate those records and prompts to
the public domain under the
[Creative Commons CC0 1.0 Universal dedication](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/legalcode).

This dedication covers only those repository-authored data records and benchmark
prompts. It does not cover model weights, generated model responses, third-party
contributions, or future datasets unless their documentation explicitly says so.
Every future record must retain its own `source` and `license` metadata.
